Map visualization

This is a visualization of meteorites hitting on the Earth. Each circle represents the location of which the meteorite is found. The opacity is reduced to better see overlaps. We can tell that the meteorites are concentrated in central/south US, Europe, North Africa, and south Austrialia.

This is a bar graph showing the top 10 countries with the most meteorite impacts. Each bar represents the occurrence of meteorite attacks within each country and they are filled with different color in order to be more distinguishable. According to the graph, we see a high occurrence of meteorite attacks in Omen. There are also a high concentration of meteorites in the US, countries in North Africa, and Australia.
